Salary Declaration

Reminder

Please note that any delay in submitting the declaration will result in a penalty of 50 DH.

Late declaration rules

  • Declaration Deadline: Salary declarations must be submitted no later than the 10th day of the declared month.
  • Late Declaration: Any declaration submitted after the deadline is considered late.
  • Late Days Calculation: Late days are calculated as the number of days between the deadline date and the actual declaration date.
  • Penalty Rate: A penalty of 1% per late day is applied to the total social contribution amount.
  • On-Time Declaration: If the declaration is submitted on or before the deadline, no penalty is applied.
